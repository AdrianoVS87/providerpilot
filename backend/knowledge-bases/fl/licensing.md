# Florida Childcare Licensing Requirements
## Regulatory Authority
- Florida Department of Children and Families (DCF)
- Chapter 402, Florida Statutes + Rule 65C-22, F.A.C.

## Key Requirements
- Family Day Care Home: 1-5 children (registration)
- Large Family Child Care Home: 6-12 children (license)
- Child Care Facility: 6+ children (license)
- Staff ratios: Infants 1:4, 1-year 1:6, 2-year 1:11, 3-year 1:15, 4+ 1:20
- Background screening: Level 2 (FBI + FDLE)
- 40-hour introductory training + 10 hours annual
- Indoor: 35 sq ft/child, Outdoor: 45 sq ft/child
- Source: Florida DCF Child Care Licensing
